== Description ==

You can add your blog to pastrank track queue by wp-pastrank, pastrank.com will track indaxed datas of different search engings and Alexa traffic rank. its founctions includes:

1.  view your analysis chart of domain datas
2.  add pastrank chart to your posts
3.  it will show the newest track datas of your blog or other domains on widget.

more information about pastrank, please visit <a href="http://www.pastrank.com/" target="_blank">www.pastrank.com</a>  
  
**What's pastrank:**

1.  View indexed datas history of search engines
2.  View alexa traffic history rank, though your alexa rank is behind 100,000
3.  View past 24h, past week, past month, past year indexed history datas of search engines
4.  View everyday data of search engines
5.  Compare different search engines together like google.com, bing.com, baidu.com etc.
6.  Completly open API,provide JSON/JSONP/Xml
7.  You can show pastrank chart to your website

  
**Who needs PastRank?**

1.  I concern indexed data of my website by different search engines.
2.  I want to know after i have done SEO, how indexed datas by search engines change
3.  I want to know indexed datas of my competitor by search engines

== Installation ==

Extract the zip file and just drop the contents in the wp-content/plugins/ directory of your WordPress installation and then activate the Plugin from Plugins page.
